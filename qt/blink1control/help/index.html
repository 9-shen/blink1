QuickStart: Connecting IFTTT to your blink(1)


IFTTT is an Internet-based service that connects other Net-based systems together.

The general steps to hook IFTTT to Blink1Control are:

  • On IFTTT, activate the blink(1) channel using your IFTTT key from Blink1Control
  • On IFTTT, create a recipe that uses the blink(1) channel as the action
  • In Blink1Control, create an IFTTT entry with the same “Rule name” as that in your IFTTT recipe.

Application Layout


The Blink1Control application has two status areas on the left and a set of control pages under tabs on the right.
